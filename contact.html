Common questions
Do I need any experience to join? ▼
Not at all. Our Basic and Teens experiences are designed for complete beginners — no experience needed whatsoever.
Can I book for a group? ▼
Yes! We love group bookings for team events, hens parties, birthdays and more. Get in touch and we'll arrange something special.
Are gift vouchers available? ▼
Absolutely. A ChattyBeans experience makes a fantastic gift. Contact us and we'll organise a voucher for any experience.
What's included in the price? ▼
Everything — all coffee, milk, equipment and materials are included. Just bring yourself and a willingness to learn!
Can parents attend the Teens session? ▼
Yes — parents and guardians are warmly welcome to sit in and watch, or even have a go themselves.
